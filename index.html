Why Decentralized?

The decentralized web (DWeb) offers a future where users control their data and content is resistant to censorship.

By hosting on the .brave TLD (and .pw), we ensure that our resources remain accessible and resilient. Zero Trust isn't just a security model; it's a philosophy of verification and independence.

Security Philosophy

"Never trust, always verify."

Zero Trust architecture abolishes the idea of a trusted network inside a corporate perimeter. We apply these principles to our educational content, teaching you how to secure systems by assuming breach and verifying every request.
