Why zero trust?

  • Empower communities with transparent, secure-by-design utilities.
  • Deliver learning journeys with rigorous access control baked in.
  • Iterate faster knowing every module can ship independently.

Mission

Privacy-first collaboration

Create safe environments for contributors by verifying every interaction and keeping sensitive data compartmentalized.

Education that scales

Equip mentors and learners with structured academies where knowledge grows without sacrificing governance or accountability.

Composable tooling

Introduce new capabilities one module at a time. Each tool in the lab integrates cleanly so you can expand when you are ready.

Tool suites you can build on today

ABC Toolkit

Automate baseline controls. The ABC toolkit provides hardened templates, checklists, and automation hooks to roll out zero-trust policies across teams with minimal friction.

  • Identity-aware access policies and playbooks
  • Scriptable guardrails for infrastructure and applications
  • Dashboards to observe adoption and compliance trends

Academie

A guided learning space for teaching zero-trust principles. Blend live workshops, labs, and certification paths to grow expertise across your community or organization.

  • Modular curriculum builder with instructor notes
  • Self-paced labs with automated validation
  • Progress analytics to coach learners effectively

Future modules

Design your roadmap and plug in additional tools as you go—ranging from incident response simulators to privacy-preserving data exchanges.

  • Space for community-developed utilities
  • Integration guidelines to keep everything interoperable
  • Open issues and ideas welcome on GitHub

Roadmap

  • Now Launch ABC and Academie pilots, collect user feedback, and refine the trust model that underpins every interaction.
  • Next Release integration kits so partners can embed identity verification, secure messaging, and consent-aware data flows.
  • Later Expand into open governance tooling with voting modules, treasury controls, and threat intelligence exchanges.
